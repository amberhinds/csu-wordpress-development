Modifying WordPress

Check out the "core" files - know what's there...

but don't change it!

When WordPress updates, your safe zone is:
  • .htaccess
  • wp-config
  • wp-content
  • any non-WP files you have in the root

Jargon & Other Things You Need To Know

"Posts" vs "Pages" ...actually everything is a post

  • "posts" = blog posts - content related to one another by category/tags (taxonomy) and by date
  • "pages" = stand-alone content that may have a hierarchical relationship to one another or none at all
  • They are all stored in the same table
